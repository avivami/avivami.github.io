About & Contact

Aviva Michaelov grew up in Tel Aviv, Israel, and graduated from Parsons School of Design. She is an art director with the @nytdesign team. Foremerly the art director of the Op-Ed page and Sunday Review section of the New York Times.

In 2010 she produced a video about the history of illustration on the Op-Ed page.

Her work at The New York Times has been recognized by the Society of Newspaper Design.

While at Parsons, Michaelov worked at multidisciplinary studio pixelpress.org as a print and web designer. After graduating she freelanced for R/GA.
